1. OVERALL ASSESSMENT
CharTuple is a faithful mirror of IntTuple (verified). Treating char as an
unsigned 16-bit numeric is sensible and the choices are well justified: min/max
/median -> char (an element; unsigned ordering correctly documented, 'Z'(90) <
'a'(97)), sum -> int (9 chars max 589,815, cannot overflow int -> no @throws,
correct), average -> double. No class-specific actionable findings.

APPLICABLE FAMILY-WIDE FINDINGS (full detail in IntTuple.txt):
  - [F1] copyOf(char[]) generic self-type return -> CCE footgun. High.
  - [F2] no arity-agnostic get(int) accessor. Medium.
  - [F3] element-destructuring accept/map/filter stop at arity 3. High.
  - [F4] empty-tuple: sum->0, min/max/median/average throw. High. KEEP.
  - Null-callback IAE(forEach) vs NPE(accept/map/filter) split. Medium.

2.-6. No CharTuple-specific findings beyond the family-wide items above.
of(8)/of(9) @Deprecated consistently (healthy).
